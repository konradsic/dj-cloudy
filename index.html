Search for anything you'd like

No need to know the right URL. Just type name of the track what you're looking for and play it! As a bonus you could select one from up to 10 search results.
Unfortunately we can currently handle non-url searches only from YouTube Music but you can also put the URL from SoundCloud and other platforms.
