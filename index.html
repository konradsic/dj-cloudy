Hop on the VC and listen to music. From anywhere.

No matter if you're travelling, playing games or working. DJ Cloudy is (almost) always available on Discord. Just /connect or /play and listen to high-quality music.
Create parties, no matter what size, or listen alone

DJ Cloudy is the perfect choice if you want to host a party on Discord, listen to music with friends or create a music event even in large servers. You can always listen alone with all permissions and a variety of commands.
Learn more.

Create playlists with songs of your liking

One of the best features of the bot is The DJ Cloudy Playlist System™. With use of commands that the system adds you can easily manage, view and play your favourite songs. No more manually adding 50 songs to the queue. Just create a playlist with a good name that you will remember like "My favourite songs", add songs and there you go - now you just need to use one command to extend the queue by your songs.
Save your favourite songs with just one click

Another feature of the Playlist System is the starred playlist. It's a special playlist, where "starred" songs go. To star a song just use the command /nowplaying. You will see a star below the embed. The "Add to starred" button is also visible when adding a song to the queue.

Configure the bot right for your needs
With many customization options for both users and guilds you can make DJ Cloudy match your needs. Configure things such as seeking, DJ roles, song filter and more!

Manage the queue with ease

Queue management was never that easy. With a lot of simple commands and built-in features you can view the queue, skip, delete etc. with just one command.
Look how easy and intuitive the commands are: /queue cleanup, /queue remove [index], /queue shuffle, /queue view, /skip, /skipto [pos]
